Berry Overnight Oats

  • Servings: 1
  • Yield: 1 serving
INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
Quaker® Oats (Quick or Old Fashioned, uncooked) 1/2 Cup
Non-fat milk 1/2 Cup
Non-fat plain Greek yogurt 1/2 Cup
Chia seeds (Optional) 1 tsp
Fresh mixed berries and fruit 1 Cup

Directions:

1. Add Quaker® Oats to your container of choice and pour in milk.

2. Layer Greek yogurt, chia seeds and mixed fruit and berries.

3. Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning!
